Our Solution

consist of ...

1. Robot

Our solution consist in the robot navigating autonomously around the shed thus stimulating the flock and making them eat and drink more.

the robot also aerates the litter.

Further more , the robot is equiped with temperature and humidity sensors and camera which report real time information of the shed and animals welfare to our mobile app. Photographs and data are processed with artificial intelligence.

2. monitor

our embedded and package system of sensors allows us to have real time data of temperature and humidity of the shack in order to cross-check it whit robot data . These fixed monitoring sensing points improve accuracy regarding the environmental measurements

3.app

All this data is uploaded in real time to our mobille & desktop app, in which you can find daily scoring, dashboards, real time photos , historic data , improvement suggestions, etc.

One of the key of components of our app are the data of the water flow meter and silo food meter, conected to our IOT accessories.

lastly, you can also upload manual information such as mortality , lot number, sex, notes and observations in order to have a complete track.

4.cameras

With top and robot views we can analyze flock behavior and with AI processes we can predict and stimate weight.
